The Problem

Global Awareness Shouldn't Be Disconnected

😤

Fragmented Data

Keeping track of global events requires monitoring dozens of disconnected sources, leading to an overwhelming signal-to-noise ratio.

⏱️

Slow Intelligence

By the time traditional news aggregates geopolitical and financial risks, the actionable window to respond has already closed.

🔧

Missing Correlation

It is incredibly difficult to spot converging trends between economic markets, military conflicts, and natural disasters.

World Monitor solves this.

A unified situational awareness interface that uses AI to synthesize over 500 news feeds and correlate cross-stream intelligence in real-time.
